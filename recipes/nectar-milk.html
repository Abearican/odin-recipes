Nectar Milk

Description

Nectar Milk is an excellect drink to have before bed. I prefer it cold, but it is also delicious if you want to heat up your milk. Incredibly simple and honestly really unhealthy. You probably should not have this but I am a fatty and a stoner and like to eat weird things.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Honey
  • Cinnamon (optional)
  • Sprayable whipped cream (optional)

Instructions

  1. If desired, drizzle some honey (or other syrup type condiment) on the inside edge of your glass.
  2. Shake together milk, cinnamon, and desired amount of honey and pour into glass.
  3. If desired, top with whipped cream, sprinkle some cinnamon on top, and serve with a straw garnished with a cinnamon stick.
